A cross-platform audio visualizer built on Electron

Download for Windows & macOS

SKQW (pronounced "skew") allows you to visualize the audio output of you computer's sound card. It is inspired by my love of the old Winamp plugins like Milkdrop and Geiss. Visualizations are written in JavaScript / GLSL and are rendered with HTML Canvas.
